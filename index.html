About Me

I moved to Chicago a couple of year ago from Phoenix, Arizona and I have enjoyed every moment of it. I'm a laid back and easy going guy who appreciates new experiences.


While I enjoy spending time with friends, being entertained, trying new things, and going new places; I'm equally happy spending time at home and curling up on the couch to watch a good movie or TV show just as much.


I'm pretty much an open book and look forward to cultivating new friendships and hopefully meeting that special someone whom I can grow with and share my life with.
